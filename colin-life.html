2025

Running
Spring 2025 · Rhode Island
First marathon. Done.
Crossed the finish line of my first full marathon — 26.2 miles along the Rhode Island coast. I started running in college mostly for fitness, then ran a half and caught the bug. Training for the full was a different animal entirely: early mornings, long Sunday runs, and a lot of learning to be comfortable with discomfort. The finish line felt like everything I'd put in over months arriving all at once. Arms up. Already thinking about the next one.

Travel
Fall 2025 · Singapore
Studying abroad at NUS Singapore.
Packed a semester into a city-state I'd never been to, on a continent I'd barely explored. The National University of Singapore has been equal parts academic challenge and personal adventure — new food, new people, new ways of thinking about engineering. Southeast Asia is right there, and I'm not taking that for granted.

Summer 2025 · Cambridge, MA
My summer at Blueprint Medicines.
Spent the summer in Cambridge at a Sanofi biotech company — building a Power BI dashboard that tracked global supply chain spend. It was my first real taste of how large organizations make decisions and how data either enables or obscures that process. Got to sit in on acquisition strategy meetings and present to senior leadership. It went well. More on this on the Work page.

Backpacking
Summer 2024 · White Mountains, NH
Leading trips into the White Mountains.
Another season as a trip leader for BC Outdoor Adventure — this time deep into the Whites. Planning a multi-day trip for a group of students who've never slept outside before is a different kind of engineering problem.
